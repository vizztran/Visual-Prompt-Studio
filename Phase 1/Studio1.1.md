**Assignment title** Visual Prompt Studio Phase 1.1 – The Sci-Fi Film Poster
**Prompt** Create a poster of a science fiction blockbuster that featuring a lone astronaut exploring an abandoned space station after receiving a mysterious distress signal. The station should have long, sharp geometric corridor lines leading the viewer’s eyes toward a glowing airlock door at the center of the composition, while curved organic pipes and cables spread across the walls to create a feeling of unease. Large rectangular hallways and circular airlock doors dominate the scene, with vast areas of dark empty space surrounding the astronaut to emphasize loneliness and isolation through the use of negative space. The walls should have rough, scratched metallic textures with signs of rust and age, while the astronaut’s suit appears smooth and reflective. The massive station should feel heavy and imposing, giving the environment a strong sense of volume and weight. Use dramatic value contrast by placing a bright white emergency light shining on the astronaut against deep black shadows, with cold blue lighting contrasted by glowing red emergency alarms to create a powerful focal point. A mysterious creature should be partially visible in the darkness, with its silhouette clearly recognizable. Its elongated limbs, glowing eyes, and unsettling outline emerge from the shadows, while most of its body remains concealed to preserve mystery and suspense. The overall mood should be tense, mysterious, isolated, and cinematic, like the poster for a blockbuster science fiction thriller.
